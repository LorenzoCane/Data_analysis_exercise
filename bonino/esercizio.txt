Exercise
Data contained in "Sun data.dat" are related to one day of Sun observations in the microwave range (1GHz = 30 cm).
The following variables have been selected:
- exposure time (downsampled to 10 seconds intervals) 
- flux_1GHz: SFU (Solar Flux Unit) 

Model the data time series.

(Original data: Nobeyama Radio Polarimeters (NoRP)
http://solar.nro.nao.ac.jp/norp/html/introduction.html)
